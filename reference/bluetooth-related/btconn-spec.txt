How to Use:

    1. Compile: gcc -o btconn btconn.c -lbluetooth

        gcc btconn.c -o btconn -lbluetooth

        gcc btconn.c -o btconn -L/usr/local/lib -lbluetooth


    2. Run: ./btconn [mac_address|csv_filename] [channel] 

        Example: ./btconn 00:11:22:33:44:55 1

        With MAC address

            ./btconn XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:XX 

        Without MAC address

            ./btconn

        With MAC Address and Channel:

            ./btconn XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:XX 3

        With CSV File:

            ./btconn devices.csv

        With CSV File and Channel:

            ./btconn devices.csv 3
